Meet Blaine

This poor little guy was rescued injured, but now is ready to find a loving home. Blaine is just a year old and as sweet and handsome as a Tabby can be. He's a little shy, but is enjoying the attention he is getting now that he's safe and well. In fact, he will give you some gentle head butts and purr to prove it. And, should you give him a treat or two, hell be one happy young fellow, but hell be even happier if you take him home.

Meet Coco Rico

Mr. Coco Rico is a friendly low-key gentleman who likes attention and will politely ask for it. He was rescued by some kind folks and is all dressed up in his tuxedo, ready to celebrate finding a real home to call his own. Coco Rico is a mature fellow who would probably be happiest in an adult home. As an added bonus, if you're over 50, his adoption fee is waived as part of the Boomer's Buddies program! Standard adoption procedures apply. To meet Coco Rico, email his foster at msarthur69@gmail.com.

Meet Farrow

Looking for a little magic, to put some spark into your life? Look no further Farrow the wizard is here to make all your dreams come true. Meet Cobain
